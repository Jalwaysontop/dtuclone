Dell Aspire Scholarship

The Dell Aspire Scholarship Program is designed to support talented students pursuing undergraduate studies in technology and engineering fields.

Key Features:

  • Financial support up to Rs. 1,20,000 per annum
  • Mentorship opportunities with Dell professionals
  • Internship opportunities at Dell Technologies
  • Access to skill development workshops
  • Networking events with industry leaders

Eligibility Criteria:

  • First-year undergraduate students
  • Family income less than 4 lakhs per annum
  • Minimum 75% marks in Class XII
  • B.Tech students only

National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

The National Scholarship Portal is a one-stop solution for all scholarship needs of students. It's a Government of India initiative to provide financial support to meritorious students.

Available Schemes:

  • Post Matric Scholarship for SC Students
  • Post Matric Scholarship for ST Students
  • Post Matric Scholarship for OBC Students
  • Merit-cum-Means Scholarship for Professional and Technical Courses
  • Central Sector Scheme of Scholarships

General Eligibility:

  • Indian nationality
  • Regular student in recognized institution
  • Family income criteria as per specific scheme
  • Valid documents and certificates
