Academic Programs at Delhi Technological University (DTU)

1. Undergraduate Programs

DTU offers a wide range of undergraduate programs across various engineering, technology, and applied sciences fields, aimed at building a solid foundation and practical knowledge base for students.

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science and Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and more.
  • Bachelor of Design (B.Des) in Design and Innovation.
  • Integrated programs combining engineering and management, focusing on interdisciplinary skills.

2. Postgraduate Programs

DTU provides a variety of postgraduate programs designed to deepen students' expertise in their chosen fields, enhance research capabilities, and foster specialized skill development.

  • Master of Technology (M.Tech) in fields such as Data Science, Power Systems, Environmental Engineering, and Signal Processing.
  • Master of Science (M.Sc) in Applied Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry.
  • MBA program with a focus on technology management and entrepreneurship.

3. Research Areas

DTU is involved in cutting-edge research across diverse areas in science, technology, and engineering. Faculty and students are encouraged to contribute to innovative solutions for real-world problems.

  • Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Robotics
  • Renewable Energy and Sustainable Development
  • Cybersecurity and Blockchain
  • Internet of Things (IoT) and Embedded Systems
  • Advanced Materials and Nanotechnology

4. Centers of Excellence

DTU hosts several Centers of Excellence, established in collaboration with industry leaders and government bodies to promote focused research and development in specific areas.

  • Center of Excellence in Artificial Intelligence
  • Center of Excellence in Sustainable Energy
  • Center for Cybersecurity and Cryptology
  • Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship
  • Center for Advanced Computing
