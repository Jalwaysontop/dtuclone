About DTU

Delhi Technological University (formerly Delhi College of Engineering) has an illustrious history spanning over 80 years. This prestigious institution is well known throughout the world for its world-class education and student-centric research & innovations.

Mode of Admission

ICCR

Under this category, the application of the candidates shall be routed directly through Indian Council of Cultural Relation (ICCR).

DTU Portal

Under this category, candidates shall apply on-line in the prescribed format before the last date directly on the web portal of the office of International Affairs.

DASA

Under this category, the student directly apply through DASA portal. Complete information regarding the procedure of admission, eligibility and payment of fee will be available on DASA website.

Frequently asked Questions

How to get admission as an international Student at DTU?

You can take admission at DTU via 3 modes: DASA, ICCR, and DTU-Portal.
