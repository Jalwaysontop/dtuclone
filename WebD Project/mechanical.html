MECHANICAL ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT

Delhi Technological University (DTU)

The Mechanical Engineering Department at Delhi Technological University (DTU) is renowned for its focus on innovation, research, and comprehensive education. With a legacy of producing industry-ready engineers, the department offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs that cover all facets of mechanical engineering, preparing students for a successful career in the industry or academia.

About the Department

The Mechanical Engineering Department at DTU emphasizes a robust theoretical foundation along with hands-on experience. Equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories and a highly qualified faculty, the department aims to foster a culture of creativity and problem-solving. Students are encouraged to engage in projects, research, and internships to apply their knowledge in real-world scenarios.

Key Features

  • Experienced faculty with expertise in various domains of mechanical engineering.
  • Advanced laboratories for Thermal Engineering, Fluid Mechanics, Robotics, and more.
  • Curriculum covering core topics such as Thermodynamics, Machine Design, Robotics, and Manufacturing.
  • Active research groups in areas such as Renewable Energy, Automotive Engineering, and Computational Fluid Dynamics.
  • Opportunities for industry collaborations, internships, and hands-on training.
  • Student-run clubs and technical societies focused on automotive design, robotics, and sustainability.

Vision

To be a leading department in mechanical engineering education and research, producing innovative and skilled engineers who contribute positively to industry and society.
