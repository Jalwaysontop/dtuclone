Electrical Engineering Department

Delhi Technological University (DTU)

The Electrical Engineering (EE) Department at Delhi Technological University (DTU) is dedicated to academic excellence, innovation, and equipping students with the skills necessary to succeed in the field of IT. The department offers programs designed to prepare students for a dynamic technological world.

About the Department

The EE department at DTU provides students with a strong foundation in core EE subjects while integrating modern advancements. With experienced faculty and advanced labs, students gain hands-on experience that fosters critical thinking and real-world problem-solving.

Key Features

  • Highly qualified faculty with specialization in diverse EE labs.
  • State-of-the-art labs.
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering topics like Power Quality, SCADA systems, IoT, Analog Electronics.
  • Research groups in areas such as Signal Processing, Smart Grid.
  • Opportunities for internships, industry projects, and global exposure.
  • Specialized knowledge on analysis and design of HVDC, Hybrid Electric Vehicles, digital signal processing along with various electives related to upcoming E.E. fields

Vision

To be a leading department in EE education and research, producing globally competent professionals who drive technological innovation and societal advancement.
