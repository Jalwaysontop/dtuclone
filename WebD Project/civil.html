Department of Civil Engineering

Delhi Technological University (DTU)

The Civil Engineering (CE) Department at Delhi Technological University (DTU) is dedicated to academic excellence, innovation, and equipping students with the skills necessary to succeed in the field of CE. The department offers programs designed to prepare students for a dynamic technological world.

About the Department

The CE department at DTU provides students with a strong foundation in core CE subjects while integrating modern advancements. With experienced faculty and advanced labs, students gain hands-on experience that fosters critical thinking and real-world problem-solving.

Key Features

  • Highly qualified faculty with specialization in diverse CE labs.
  • State-of-the-art labs.
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering topics like Water Resources Engineering, River Water Quality Modelling .
  • Research groups in areas such as Structural Engineering, Structural Dynamics, Earthquake Engineering, Traffic planning.
  • Opportunities for internships, industry projects, and global exposure.
  • Specialized knowledge on Stochastic modelling and applications of Artificial Intelligence in Civil Engineering, along with various electives related to upcoming Civil fields

Vision

To be a leading department in CE education and research, producing globally competent professionals who drive technological innovation and societal advancement.
